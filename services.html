Our Services

Precision solutions for every metalwork challenge.

Comprehensive Metalworking Capabilities

At Chavez Sheet Metal, we offer a full spectrum of services designed to meet the rigorous demands of commercial, industrial, and residential projects. Our state-of-the-art facility and expert team are ready to bring your project to life.

Custom Sheet Metal Fabrication

We transform your designs into tangible, high-quality metal components. From complex prototypes to large-scale production runs, our fabrication process is defined by precision and attention to detail.

  • Laser Cutting & CNC Punching
  • Bending, Forming & Rolling
  • Stainless Steel, Aluminum & Mild Steel

Industrial Ductwork

Our team specializes in fabricating and installing robust, efficient ductwork systems for industrial ventilation, dust collection, and material handling. We ensure optimal airflow and system longevity.

  • HVAC & Exhaust Systems
  • Fume & Dust Extraction
  • Spiral and Rectangular Ducts

Built to Last. Engineered to Perform.

Welding & Repair

Our AWS-certified welders provide flawless structural and cosmetic welds across a range of materials. We also offer on-site and in-house repair services to restore the integrity of your equipment and components.

  • MIG, TIG & Stick Welding
  • Structural Steel & Pipe Welding
  • On-Site Mobile Welding Services

HVAC Metal Components

We are a trusted supplier for HVAC contractors, providing custom-fabricated components that ensure efficient and reliable system performance. All parts are made to precise specifications for a perfect fit.

  • Plenums, Drain Pans & Fittings
  • Rooftop Curbs & Equipment Stands
  • Custom Flashing & Enclosures

Design & Consulting

Leverage our expertise before fabrication begins. Our team can assist with design for manufacturability (DFM), material selection, and project planning to optimize cost, timeline, and final product quality.

  • CAD/CAM Design Services
  • Prototyping & Product Development
  • Project Cost & Feasibility Analysis
