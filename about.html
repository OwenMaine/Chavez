Our Story

From a Small Workshop to an Industry Leader

Chavez Sheet Metal LLC was founded in 2005 by master craftsman Alejandro Chavez. What began as a one-man operation fueled by a passion for precision metalwork has grown into one of Maryland's most respected fabrication shops.

Our mission is simple: to deliver exceptional craftsmanship and durable solutions through innovation, integrity, and a relentless commitment to our clients' success. We believe every project is a partnership, and we bring decades of combined experience to ensure your vision is realized to the highest standard.
